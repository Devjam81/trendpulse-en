The new star of Chinese athletics: Zheng Qinwen

The exceptional performances of a young Chinese athlete have shaken the minds! Zheng Qinwen, 20 years old, has shaken the sports community with his crushing results. In this article, we will introduce you to this sprint prodigy, his burgeoning career, and the reasons that make him one of the athletes to watch.

Rapid rise to the top

Zheng Qinwen started practicing athletics at the age of 10. Despite an initial specialization in combined events, he eventually opted for sprinting and never looked back. Since then, he has won over 20 Chinese national titles and broken several national records.

Phenomenal performances

In 2021, Zheng Qinwen participated in his first Asian championship and won the silver medal in the 100 meters. The following year, he further improved his performance by winning the Chinese national title in the 200 meters and setting a new national record with a time of 20.36 seconds.

International competition

Zheng Qinwen is part of a new generation of athletes who are making a name for themselves in the world of athletics.
