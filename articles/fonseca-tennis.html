A breakout talent with rare early results

He finished the 2024 season with a 7-7 ATP match record, which is notable for an 18-year-old competing at that level.[2] He also became the youngest player to qualify for the ATP Next Gen Finals, a tournament reserved for top under-20 men’s players.[2]
