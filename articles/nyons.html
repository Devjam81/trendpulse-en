The Nyons, a trend that shines!

The Nyons are back in force! After experiencing a resurgence of interest in recent years, these wide and flat-framed glasses have taken over fashion plates and social media. But what are The Nyons? And why is this vintage trend becoming so popular?

History and style

The Nyons first appeared over 70 years ago, created by German optician F.A. Steinle in 1952. At the time, they were designed to protect outdoor workers' eyes from the sun's rays and airborne particles. But it's their unique design that really made them stand out: wide and flat frames, with a characteristic "T" shape.

Retro trend

The Nyons have experienced a resurgence of interest in recent years, particularly among younger generations. Why? It's perhaps because these glasses evoke a simpler, happier time. The Nyons are synonymous with freedom, nature, and outdoor life. They are part of a slower, more relaxed world, where people stop to admire the view.

Fashion and culture

The Nyons have also gained ground in the fashion world. They are worn by celebrities and influencers, who combine them with their minimalist or more flashy outfits. Fashion brands have even created their own versions of Nyons, often with touches of luxury and sophistication.
