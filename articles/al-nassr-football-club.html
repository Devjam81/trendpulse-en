A Rich History

Al-Nassr FC has a long history dating back over 65 years. Founded in 1955 by a group of amateurs, the team has undergone rapid development under the leadership of its first president, Prince Faisal bin Fahd. Today, Al-Nassr is considered one of the oldest and most respected teams in the Arab world.
